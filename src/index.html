ABOUT US

Nord Star Trading, Ltd. is a young but prospective international company formed by enthusiastic professionals with numerous years of experience in supplying equipment, machines, spare parts and other materials from all the corners of the world to any country the client needs.

We are working directly with the factories of the world-wide known brands, like Cummins, CAT, JCB, Hyundai, Terex, Shantui, Wuxi, Sinomud, DongFeng, Everdigm and the others.

Our main activities are related to the supply of:

  • mining machines (bulldozers, drilling rigs, excavators, dump trucks, cranes, manipulators, loaders, etc.)
  • equipment (mining factories equipment; crushing equipment; gold-mining laboratory equipment; geological, geophysics and drilling equipment, tools and materials)
  • spare parts
  • chemicals & oils (greases; borax; lime; flocculent; soda ash; activated carbon; cyanide, etc.)
  • other associated materials

We can offer you A, B and C quality class spare parts, just according to your needs.
